What is a peer-to-peer support network?

In a peer-to-peer support network, patients, caregivers and health advocates connect online with guidance from credible organizations and institutions. Peer support is the mutual exchange of help and conversation based on a shared understanding, respect and mutual empowerment between people in similar situations.
